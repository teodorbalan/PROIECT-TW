Intellectual Pursuits & Professional Growth

My intellectual journey has been driven by a fascination with the intersection of AI and medicine. This was ignited at **Stanford's Neuroengineering course**, where I developed a machine-learning program to predict Palsy disease in children. Following this, I collaborated on a research project at **Columbia and John Hopkins**, using K-means clustering to analyze the behavior of ganglion cells. These experiences strengthened my resolve to contribute to medical advancements through AI.

My professional growth started at **Soft31**, a software company in Romania, where I was the youngest programmer. I quickly learned multiple languages and collaborated with colleagues to build a machine-learning app that connects symptoms to diseases. This project was a major milestone, as our program was acquired by Regina Maria, a premier private hospital network in Romania.

Extracurricular Activities & Community

My commitment to using my skills for good is demonstrated through my volunteering with **KAIA ONG**. I started by teaching English and Romanian to Ukrainian refugee children and later took the initiative to organize cultural outings to help them feel welcomed and integrated into our society. I believe in giving back to the community and helping the less fortunate.

For six years, I was a member of the **Nerdvana robotics team**, where I was the lead programmer. My expertise in computer vision and data analysis allowed me to create a sophisticated PID algorithm that greatly enhanced our robot's navigation. We qualified for the World Robotics Olympiad multiple times, achieving a remarkable 8th-place finish in 2022.
